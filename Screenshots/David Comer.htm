Profile

I recently spent 4 years in sales and consultancy, working in teams, thinking on my feet and using logistical skills to solve client problems.
For the last few years, I've been a consultant in private equity tech, where I became completely hooked on development. I loved being able to see tangible outputs of my creative work so much that I decided to dedicate my efforts fully into learning to code properly and making it my career, so I enrolled at the Web Development Course at General Assembly. The course at GA completely immersed me in the constructive programming environment that I had only been partially exposed to in my previous role and gave me a profound knowledge of the technical skills i will need to embark on a career in web development.
I’d most love to work at a well-organised, collaborative company in a friendly and creative team.
